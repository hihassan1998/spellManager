About Us

This Yahtzee game is an object-oriented Python project that implements the game's international rules and logic. The goal is to practice design patterns, class management, and algorithm efficiency through a practical application of object-oriented principles.

Game Rules:
Ones: Sum of all ones rolled.
Twos: Sum of all twos rolled.
Threes: Sum of all threes rolled.
Fours: Sum of all fours rolled.
Fives: Sum of all fives rolled.
Sixes: Sum of all sixes rolled.
Three of a Kind: If at least three dice show the same number, sum of all dice.
Four of a Kind: If at least four dice show the same number, sum of all dice.
Full House: Three of one number and two of another (e.g., 3-3-3-2-2) → 25 points.
Small Straight: Four consecutive numbers (e.g., 1-2-3-4) → 30 points.
Large Straight: Five consecutive numbers (e.g., 2-3-4-5-6) → 40 points.
Yahtzee: All five dice show the same number → 50 points.
Chance: Sum of all dice (any combination).
